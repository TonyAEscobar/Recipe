Ingredients

- Head of Lettuce -

- Carrot -

- Bowl of Choice -

- Dressing -

- Knife -

- Carrot Peeler -

- Broccoli -

- Cauliflour -

The first step is to cut the Lettuce Head in half, we are going to only use half of the head. Cut out the middle of the head and throw it away, we are not going to be using it. Cut up the lettuce until it is in small pieces, and place them into your desired bowl.



With a knife, cut off both pieces of the carrot's ends. Use the peeler to peel off the skin into a bowl or sink.



After you have peeled off the carrot's skin, use the peeler to peel off slices into the bowl that you placed the lettuce in.


Cut off the tops off the Broccoli and Cauliflour and then put the tops into the bowl along with the lettuce and carrot slices.



For the final step, stir around all of your ingredients and then place your desired topping (mine was ranch) onto your salad. Take a fork and then stir your topping into the salad.
